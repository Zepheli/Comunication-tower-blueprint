Comunication tower

A machine that able to transport sound and long destant messages. This machine will have to be tuned to a specific frequency. All of those that will have comunicator devices with that frequency will be able to comunicate with each other.

The comunication tower will be constructed out of adamantite and tyrium with orichalcum and copper wiring an a a lot of wind and lightning crystals.

The comunicator devices will be made out of a core of wind and lightning crystals and mitheril casing and some copper and orichalcum circuitry.
